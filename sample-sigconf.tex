\begin{abstract}
%establish a clear reason why this research is important
% constant terminology - textual discussions
The software development process might have the probability to be influenced by how engineers communicate through their collaborative platforms, how politely they talk and how they choose their words. However, to study such questions we first need a reliable automated technique that can process the languages found in these platforms, and give reasonable ratings. In our study, we evaluate popular existing tools for “sentiment” and “politeness” analysis over a dataset of GitHub comments. We identified the best sentiment detection tool, evaluated one politeness marker tool and verified in what aspects do they give an acceptable result and where they fail. We also developed an annotation scheme to rate on politeness for texts in GitHub community
\end{abstract}
